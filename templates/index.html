About Us

This website was created by three students from Sfax Engineering School - Oussama Slimani, Siwar Gouirah, and Iheb Charfeddine. Its main goal is to help doctors quickly identify brain tumors. The website allows doctors to easily upload and analyze MRI images. The main aim is to make the diagnostic process faster and more accurate, which can help doctors detect brain problems sooner. The website is designed to improve the efficiency of medical professionals, making sure that patients with possible neurological issues receive timely treatment.

Effortlessly detects brain tumors with a user-friendly interface for easy MRI image uploads.

  • 01 Upload the images
  • 02Receive a message indicating whether the uploaded image shows a brain tumor or not
  • 03If the message indicates a brain tumor, there is an option to perform segmentation on the images.
